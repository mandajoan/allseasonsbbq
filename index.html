A Little About All Seasons BBQ

Family owned and operated, we are a business who loves to boast everything we cook is made from scratch! Our signature BBQ sauce is our pride and joy, and we take its preparation very seriously.

We have been lucky enough to serve names such as Nestle Corp., the USC Football team, UCLA Medical Staff, and are very proud to say the United States Military for over 10 years.

In addition to our catering services, our BBQ truck can be found in Downtown Los Angeles (7th & Figueroa) on Tuesdays & Thursdays, 10am - 4pm. We are at the Northridge Farmers Market on Wednesdays, 5 - 9pm. We hope to see you there!

Sophisticated and Tasty Catering Services

We are available for lunch drop-offs, special events, on-sight cooking, and any other catering need!

BBQ Menu

  • Meat Selections: Beef Ribs, Pork Ribs, Baby Back Ribs, St. Louis Ribs, Tri-Tip, Beef Links & Hot Dogs, Chicken, Carne Asada, Rib-Eye Steaks, New York Steaks, Top Sirloin, Hamburgers (Vegetarian Burgers)
  • Side Selections: Baked Beans, Potato Salad, Cole Slaw, Corn on the Cob, Tossed Green Salad, Macaroni Salad, Pasta Salad, Ceaser Salad

Traditional Menu

  • Meat Selections: Turkey with Cornbread Dressing, Glazed Ham, Baked or Fried Cornish Hens with Wild Rice Dressing, Roasted Beef, Pork, or Lamb.
  • Side Selections: Mashed Potatoes with Gravy, Rice with Gravy, Macaroni & Cheese, Green Beans, Spinach, Peas, Greens, Fruit Salad, Monkey Bread, Cranberry Sauce, Rhubarb Sauce, and Apple Sauce.

Soul Food Menu
